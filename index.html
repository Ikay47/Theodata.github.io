About Me

I'm a passionate public health doctor and data scientist with extensive experience driving data-informed health interventions in Nigeria. Over the past 5+ years, I've supported U.S. CDC-funded HIV programs, using advanced analytics, dashboards, and digital tools to improve patient outcomes and strengthen program performance.

I specialize in data analysis, visualization, and machine learning, with hands-on expertise in Python, R, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau. My work spans developing predictive models, automating reporting systems, and mentoring healthcare teams on data-driven decision-making.

When I'm not optimizing health systems with data, you can find me building innovative digital solutions, teaching aspiring analysts, or exploring new frontiers in AI and public health informatics.
